Accessibility


This website has several accessibility options. One of which is alt tags on the images to tell a screen reader what the image is of. Another thing is the colour scheme used, this is to help colour-blind people read and understand my page.


If you are having problems in viewing certain parts of the webpage, I suggest to update your browser, and or, use a different browser.


Here at My Website, we strive to develope the site in accordance with the W3C guidelines.


If you have any problems in using the website, please do not hesitate to contact me here
